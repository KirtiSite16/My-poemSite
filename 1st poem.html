The Quiet Between Us

Theme: Unspoken love, knowing he loves someone else
I love you, but the words get stuck, Behind my smile, inside my luck. You laugh, and I just look away— How could I speak? What could I say? You talk of her, eyes full of light, While I dissolve into the night. Each moment near you feels so wide, But I’m just standing on the side. I write your name in silent air, A ghost of hope I shouldn’t wear. You never knew, and never will— My heart, it breaks but must stay still. This love’s a room I cannot leave, A quiet ache I cannot grieve.
